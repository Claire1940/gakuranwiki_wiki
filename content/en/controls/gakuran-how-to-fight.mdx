<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Brawling Progression Guide">

Transitioning from a passive observer to a dominant schoolyard fighter requires a structured approach to practice. Do not expect to win every encounter immediately. Instead, treat every defeat as a lesson in spacing, timing, and defensive discipline.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Your Spacing">
    Before throwing a single punch, learn to maintain a safe distance from your opponent. Move back and forth just outside their maximum striking range to bait out an early attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Block and Parry">
    Hold your block key when an opponent approaches. If you time your block right as their strike lands, you will trigger a parry, briefly stunning them and opening a window for a counter-strike.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Short, Safe Combos">
    Do not commit to long, flashy combo sequences initially. Stick to short, two-hit light punch strings followed by an immediate dash away to keep yourself safe from counter-attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Incorporate Heavy Attacks">
    Once your opponent begins holding their block defensively, mix in a heavy attack to shatter their guard, leaving them completely vulnerable to your highest-damage style moves.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Mindless Chasing">
Never chase an opponent in a straight line down hallways or stairs. Linear movement makes your approach incredibly predictable, allowing defensive players to easily time a dash-in parry or a heavy counter-strike.
</Callout>

To help you practice your offensive strings, use the standardized combo sequences below. These inputs are designed to maximize your damage output while minimizing your vulnerability if the opponent manages to block your initial strike.

| Combo Type | Input Sequence | Execution Timing | Strategic Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Full Punish** | Parry -> Light -> Light -> Heavy | Instant reaction after parry | Maximize damage during opponent stun |
