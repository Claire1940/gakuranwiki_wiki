<Section icon={Swords} title="Gakuran Combat Controls and Default Keybinds">

To survive your first encounter, you must commit the default **gakuran combat controls** to muscle memory. The game runs on the Roblox engine, utilizing a custom combat layer that requires players to manually toggle their combat readiness. 

The most important input is the Combat Stance toggle. Without activating your stance, your character cannot execute attacks, block incoming strikes, or perform advanced evasive maneuvers.

| Action | Keybind | Tactical Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Combat Stance | R | Prepares your guard and enables offensive inputs |
| Light Attack | M1 / Left Click | Fast damage, ideal for starting combo strings |
| Heavy Attack | M2 / Right Click | Slow, high-damage strike that can break guards |
| Block / Parry | F | Negates incoming basic attacks when timed correctly |
| Dodge / Dash | Q | Quickly reposition to avoid heavy attacks |
| Sprint | Double Tap W | Closes the distance or helps you escape brawls |
| Carry | B | Transports knocked down opponents out of danger |
| Execute / Grab | V | Eliminates defeated opponents to secure points |
| Map Marker | P | Marks locations to coordinate with your crew |

For players using alternative platforms, Gakuran translates these inputs to suit controllers and touchscreens. While PC remains the preferred platform for precise timing, mobile and console setups are fully viable with proper layout adjustments.

| Action | Controller Input | Mobile Input |

To win consistently, you must master the art of baiting. Walk just within your opponent's attack range, quickly dash backward using your dodge key, and then immediately move back in with a light attack sequence while they are locked in their recovery animation.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Combat Progression for Beginners">

If you are new to school brawling, jumping straight into crowded areas will likely result in frustration. Follow this structured progression path to build your skills safely.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Configure Client Settings">
    Log into Gakuran, open your Roblox settings, and set your camera mode to Classic. Ensure Shift Lock is enabled and adjust your graphics volume to keep your frame rate smooth during chaotic fights.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Practice Combat Stance Toggling">
    Find a quiet corner of the map and practice pressing **R** to enter your combat stance. Get used to the transition speed and ensure you can toggle it instantly when an opponent approaches.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Block and Dash Timing">
    Spar with a friend or a low-pressure opponent. Focus entirely on holding **F** to block their light attacks, and practice pressing **Q** to dash sideways the moment they wind up a heavy attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Basic Punishes">
    Once you can successfully block or dodge an opponent's attack, immediately follow up with a three-hit light attack string. Do not overextend; reset your positioning after the third hit.
  </Step>
</Steps>
