<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step Combat Strategy">

Entering a fight in Gakuran Roblox RP without a plan is the fastest way to end up in a "knocked" state. The combat system rewards players who can read their opponent's animations rather than those who simply spam clicks. Effective combat is a cycle of baiting, parrying, and punishing.

Follow these steps to improve your win rate in open-world school brawls:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate Combat Stance">
    Never approach an enemy without pressing **R**. This readies your character for immediate blocking (F) or dashing (Q). Walking into a fight in passive mode makes you a target for a "free" heavy attack opener.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage the Gap (Spacing)">
    Use your dash (Q) to stay just outside the range of your opponent's M1 strikes. Your goal is to make them "whiff" (miss) an attack. A missed heavy attack (M2) leaves the opponent vulnerable for nearly a full second.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Perfect Block">
    Pressing **F** just as an attack is about to land triggers a parry. This stuns the opponent and allows you to start your own combo string without them being able to dash away immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute and Reposition">
    Once an opponent is downed, use **V** to execute or **B** to carry them to a secure location. Always check your surroundings after a knockdown, as "third-party" players often wait nearby to jump into the fray.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Stamina Management">
Watch your stamina bar! Dashing and heavy attacks consume significant energy. If you run out of stamina, you cannot block effectively, leaving you completely defenseless against a Muay Thai rush.
</Callout>

</Section>
