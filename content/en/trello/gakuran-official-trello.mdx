<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering PvP Controls and Mechanics">

The barrier between a "freshman" and a "senior" in Gakuran is mastery of the control scheme. Unlike standard simulators, this experience utilizes the full Roblox control layer to allow for nuanced movement. You must toggle your combat stance (R) before engaging, or you will find yourself unable to block incoming strikes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable Combat Stance">
    Press **R** to enter your fighting stance. This changes your movement speed and enables the use of M1 and M2 attacks. Never walk into a hostile zone in "passive" mode.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Practice the Dash-Cancel">
    Use **Q** to dash. You can use this to close the gap or disengage. Learning to dash *sideways* rather than just backward is the key to avoiding heavy attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Block/Parry">
    Hold **F** to block. If you time your block exactly as an attack hits, you may trigger a parry, leaving the opponent open for a full Muay Thai or Boxing combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute and Carry">
    Once an opponent is downed, use **V** to execute or **B** to carry. In group fights, carrying an opponent to a safer area before finishing them prevents their teammates from reviving them.
  </Step>
</Steps>
