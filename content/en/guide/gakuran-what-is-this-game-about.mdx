<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Survival">

Combat in Gakuran is not just about mashing the attack button. It is a game of "turns." If you hit an opponent's block, it is usually their turn to counter-attack. Understanding the "Parry" mechanic is what separates the veterans from the "freshies." A perfect parry (blocking just before an attack hits) will stun your opponent, opening them up for a full combo.

**Essential Combat Habits:**
- **Don't hold W**: Walking forward constantly makes you predictable. Use side-dashes (Q + A/D) to circle your opponent.
- **Watch Stamina**: Every dash and heavy attack consumes energy. If you run out, you cannot dodge, making you a sitting duck.
- **Confirm your hits**: Only commit to a heavy attack (M2) if you have already landed two or three light attacks (M1).
