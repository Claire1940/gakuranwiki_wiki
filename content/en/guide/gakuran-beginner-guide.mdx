<Section icon={Target} title="Core Controls and Keybinds Setup">

Before engaging in any combat, configuring your control scheme is vital. Gakuran runs within the Roblox engine, meaning your foundational movement relies on standard platform mechanics. However, the game implements custom layers for combat stances, dashes, and heavy attacks that you must master to compete effectively.

The most critical setting to enable immediately upon loading in is **Shift Lock**. Without Shift Lock, tracking moving targets becomes incredibly difficult, as your character will not automatically face the direction of your camera. You can toggle this setting in the default Roblox escape menu under the "Settings" tab.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Basics and PvP Strategy">

Combat in Gakuran is fast, unforgiving, and highly mechanical. Unlike standard roleplay games where combat is secondary, here it is a core pillar. To succeed, you must move away from aggressive button-mashing and instead adopt a methodical approach based on counter-plays, spacing, and defensive discipline.

Every engagement begins with your stance. Walking around with your guard down makes you an easy target for roaming players. By activating your combat stance, you signal readiness and unlock your character's defensive options, such as blocking and dodging.

| Action | PC Input | Controller Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Stance** | R | D-Pad Down | Essential to activate before fighting |
| **Light Attack** | M1 / Left Click | R2 | Fast punch or kick chains |
| **Heavy Attack** | M2 / Right Click | Y | High damage, breaks blocks |
| **Dodge / Dash** | Q | B | Quick repositioning tool |
| **Block / Parry** | F | L2 | Reduces or negates incoming damage |
| **Execute / Grab** | V | L1 + X | Finishes downed opponents |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Activate Combat Stance">
    Press R immediately when entering a high-risk area. This readies your character's posture, allowing you to instantly block or dash when attacked.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Spacing and Movement">
    Avoid running directly at opponents in straight lines. Use side-dashes (Q + directional keys) to cut angles and bait out missed attacks from your opponent.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Implement Block and Parry Habits">
    Hold F to block incoming light attacks. If you time your block perfectly right before an attack lands, you can parry the strike, leaving the opponent vulnerable to a counter-attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Reliable Short Combos">
    Do not attempt complex, high-risk combinations early on. Stick to three light attacks (M1) followed by a quick dash away to reset your positioning and maintain safety.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="The Power of Patience">
The most common mistake new players make is chasing opponents while continuously swinging. This quickly drains your stamina and leaves you open to easy parries. Let your opponent commit to an attack first, block or dodge it, and then execute your counter-strike.
</Callout>
