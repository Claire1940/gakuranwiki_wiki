<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Mechanics: The Art of the Brawl">

The combat in Gakuran is more than just clicking until someone falls over. It utilizes a "Stance" system that dictates your vulnerability. This section of our **gakuran tutorial** breaks down the professional combat loop used by the top-ranked players in the community.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter Combat Stance (R)">
    Never walk into a fight in "Idle" mode. Pressing **R** changes your animations and readies your character for action. This is the only state where you can effectively use the Block (F) and Dash (Q) mechanics.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage Your Spacing">
    Do not run directly at your opponent. Use "Diagonal Movement" (holding W and A/D) to circle them. This makes it harder for them to land a Heavy Attack (M2) while you look for an opening.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Block-Parry Loop">
    Hold **F** to block basic M1 attacks. If you time your block perfectly as the hit lands, you perform a **Parry**, which briefly stuns the opponent and gives you a "True Combo" window to counter-attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute and Finish">
    Once an opponent's health is depleted, they will enter a "Downed" state. Use **B** to carry them if you are roleplaying, or **V** to execute a finisher. Be careful—executing in public areas often draws the attention of nearby gangs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Stamina Management">
Dashing and blocking consume stamina. If your stamina bar hits zero, your block will "break," leaving you wide open for a Heavy Attack. Always disengage and back off if your bar is below 25%.
</Callout>

</Section>
