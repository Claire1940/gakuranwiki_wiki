<Section icon={Target} title="Gakuran Game Controls and Keybinds">

Success in the **Gakuran game** depends heavily on muscle memory. Unlike casual roleplay games, the combat system requires precise timing for blocks and dodges. The game utilizes the standard Roblox engine but overlays a custom combat layer that players must toggle manually to engage in fights.

The following table breaks down the essential inputs for PC, Mobile, and Controller players.

| Action | PC Keybind | Mobile Input | Controller |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Combat Stance** | **R** | On-screen Toggle | **L3 / Stick Press** |
| **Light Attack** | **M1 (Left Click)** | Tap Screen | **R2 / RT** |
| **Heavy Attack** | **M2 (Right Click)** | Long Press | **Y / Triangle** |
| **Block / Parry** | **F** | Shield Icon | **L2 / LT** |
| **Dodge / Dash** | **Q** | Directional Swipe | **B / Circle** |
| **Sprint** | **Double Tap W** | Joystick Extension | **L3** |
| **Execute / Grab** | **V** | Context Button | **L1 + X** |
| **Carry Player** | **B** | Context Button | **X / Square** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Mechanic">
You cannot block or dash effectively unless you are in **Combat Stance (R)**. Walking around with your hands down makes you a "free target" for experienced players.
</Callout>

Movement is just as important as striking. Players often make the mistake of "holding W" (moving forward constantly), which makes their attacks predictable. Effective brawlers use the **Q key** to dash laterally, creating angles that bypass an opponent's block.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Guide: From Beginner to Delinquent">

Entering your first fight in the **Gakuran game** can be overwhelming. The "stun" mechanics mean that if you get hit once, you are likely to get hit three more times unless you know how to "Escape" or "Counter-Parry." Follow these steps to improve your win rate in the school hallways.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the 'Perfect Parry'">
    Don't just hold the **F key**. Wait for the opponent's arm to move forward, then tap F. A successful parry stuns the opponent, giving you a free window for a full combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage Your Stamina">
    Sprinting, dashing, and heavy attacks consume stamina. If your bar hits zero, you move slower and cannot block. Always back off when your stamina is below 25%.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Learn the 'M1-M1-M1-M2' Combo">
    The most basic but effective string is three light attacks followed by a heavy attack. The heavy attack acts as a "guard break" if they are blocking the lights.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Use the Environment">
    In the **Gakuran game**, you can pin players against lockers or walls. This limits their dash options and makes it easier to land "Grab" (V) finishers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid 'W-Spamming'">
Experienced players will wait for you to run at them and simply tap the parry key. Mix up your movement by walking backward or dashing sideways to bait out their block early.
</Callout>

</Section>
