<Section icon={Trophy} title="Combat Mechanics and PvP Strategy">

Surviving open-world PvP in Gakuran requires more than just button mashing. The combat system rewards patience, spacing, and smart defensive play [1]. If you run into a fight swinging wildly, experienced players will easily block your attacks and punish you with a high-damage counter-combo [1].

To improve your combat efficiency, focus on these core PvP strategies:

- **Stance Discipline**: Always enter your combat stance before approaching an opponent to ensure your blocks and dashes are responsive [1].
- **Dash Spacing**: Use your dash key to cut angles around your opponent. Dash backward to bait an attack, then dash forward to punish their miss [1].
- **Block and Parry**: Hold your block key when an opponent starts a combo. If you time your block perfectly as their attack lands, you can execute a parry, opening them up for a counter-attack [1].
- **Stamina Management**: Do not spam your dash key. Running out of stamina leaves you slow and unable to dodge heavy attacks [1].
