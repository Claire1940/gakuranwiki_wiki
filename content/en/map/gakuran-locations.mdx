<Section icon={Swords} title="High-Risk Combat Zones and PvP Hotspots">

While the game is framed as a slice-of-life experience, the community culture leans heavily toward Battlegrounds-style fighting. Certain **gakuran locations** have become infamous for being "death traps" where players are likely to be attacked on sight. Mastering these zones involves understanding line-of-sight, exit routes, and how to use the environment to break an opponent's combo.

The Hallways and Entrances are particularly dangerous due to their narrow nature. In these choke points, it is incredibly easy for a group to corner a solo player, leaving no room for the "dash" or "dodge" mechanics to function effectively. Conversely, the Town Streets offer more room for movement but leave you vulnerable to long-range pursuit and multi-person ambushes.

| Zone Type | Hazard Level | Survival Strategy | Key Danger |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Narrow Hallways** | ★★★★★ | Keep your back to a door | Corner trapping |

Success in these zones often depends on your ability to disengage. In the open streets, use the corners of buildings to break the camera lock of your opponent.
