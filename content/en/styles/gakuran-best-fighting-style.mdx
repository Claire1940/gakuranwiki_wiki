Success in Gakuran requires more than just picking a style; it demands mastery of core combat mechanics. Understanding how to manage your space and timing is the difference between winning a fight and becoming a training dummy.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter Combat Stance">
    Always press R (PC) or the designated input to enter combat stance before engaging. This readies your character for blocking and parrying.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Spacing">
    Use dash (Q) to reposition. Never chase enemies in a straight line, as this makes you an easy target for counter-attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Block and Punish">
    Hold F to block incoming chains. Wait for the opponent to exhaust their stamina or whiff an attack before you begin your counter-combo.
