### Deep Dive: Muay Thai (The Aggressive King)
Muay Thai dominates the current 2026 meta due to its ability to maintain forward momentum. In Gakuran, if you stop attacking, you are likely being attacked. Muay Thai minimizes this downtime with aggressive kick-based strikes that can break through an opponent's guard if they panic-block.

### Deep Dive: Boxing (The Reliable Standard)
Boxing remains the backbone of the Gakuran player base. It offers clean punch chains that are easy to practice in the gym or basketball court. While it lacks the flashy "flash" of other styles, its fundamental strength lies in its simplicity.

| Style | Strength | Weakness | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Muay Thai** | Forward pressure / Kicks | Reckless chasing | Aggressive brawlers |
| **Boxing** | Clean punch chains | Short range | New players |
| **Hakari** | Unique identity | Hard to master | Creative specialists |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Mechanics and PvP Guide">

Combat in Gakuran is more than just clicking until someone falls. It is a dance of stamina, spacing, and psychological warfare. The difference between a veteran delinquent and a "training dummy" is how they handle the combat stance and the parry window.

### The Importance of Combat Stance
Entering your combat stance (typically the **R** key on PC) is the first thing you must do before engaging. This changes your movement profile and readies your defensive options. Fighting without being in stance is a guaranteed way to lose a trade.

### Spacing and Movement
Spacing decides whether your combo starts cleanly or ends in public embarrassment. You should never chase an opponent in a straight line; instead, cut angles around the school's architecture—stairs, entrances, and corners.

| Combat Action | Primary Purpose | Key Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blocking** | Damage mitigation | Don't panic-block from far away |
| **Parrying** | Opening a punish window | Time it for the end of a chain |
| **Dashing** | Repositioning / Dodging | Keep an exit path behind you |
| **Executing** | Finishing the opponent | Only use when the area is clear |

<Callout type="warning" title="PvP Warning">
Hallways and school entrances are high-risk zones. Doorways often become ambush funnels where groups will wait to trap lone players. Always keep your camera active and rotate it 360 degrees.
</Callout>
