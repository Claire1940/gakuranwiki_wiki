### The In-Game Social Phone System
The in-game phone serves as your command center. In May 2007 Japan, Mixi was the dominant social networking platform, and the game honors this setting by using the phone for player connections. By pressing the toggle key, you can pull out your device to view your active stats, coordinate with your gang, check job boards, or buy refreshments from vending machines. Staying connected is crucial because solo players are easy targets for rival school gangs.

### Defining \"The Mix\" in Combat
In Gakuran, \"getting mixed\" or \"putting the mix on someone\" refers to executing unpredictable combat mix-ups. Because the game is heavily parry-based, spamming light attacks (M1) will quickly lead to your attacks being perfect-blocked. To break an opponent's defense, you must mix light attacks, heavy guard-breaks (M2), dodges, and grabs.

| Action | PC Input | Controller Input | Combat Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Combat Stance** | R | D-Pad Up | Activates fighting mode and prepares defense |
| **Light Attack** | M1 (Click) | R2 | Fast punch or kick chains to build pressure |
| **Heavy Attack** | M2 (Right Click) | Y | Ragdolls on hit, deals high damage, breaks guard |
| **Block / Parry** | F | L2 | Perfect block (parry) stuns attacking opponents |
| **Dodge / Dash** | Q | B | Closes or creates distance, evades heavy attacks |
| **Toggle Phone** | L / Left Toggle | L3 | Accesses social menu, gang coordinates, and jobs |

The game features a wide range of heights, from short midget builds to towering giants. Each build has distinct pros and cons that require different tactical approaches in PvP.

### Choosing the Right Build for Your Style
Your height directly links to your effectiveness with specific fighting styles. For example, if you are running a kick-heavy style like Hakari, a tall build is highly recommended because it extends the reach of your kick hitboxes. Conversely, if you prefer fast close-range punch combos like Boxing, a shorter build allows you to move quickly, weave through attacks, and exploit your smaller hitbox.
