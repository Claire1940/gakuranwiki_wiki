<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step PvP Progression Guide">

Transitioning from a fresh player to a competent schoolyard brawler takes systematic practice. Rather than jumping directly into crowded hallway fights, beginners should follow a structured progression path. This ensures you build fundamental skills, understand frame data, and learn how to punish opponent mistakes before testing your limits against experienced players.

Progression in Gakuran is not just about choosing a style; it is about developing situational awareness. Group fights are common, and understanding how to manage multiple targets while maintaining your stamina is what separates amateur fighters from top-tier brawlers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable Combat Stance">
    Before committing to any attacks, press R to enter your combat stance. This readies your character for immediate movement, blocking, and dodging.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Spacing and Dashes">
    Practice dashing in and out of range using the Q key. Learn the maximum reach of your chosen style's light attacks to avoid whiffing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Practice Block and Punish">
    Hold F to block incoming strikes. Wait for the opponent to finish their attack chain or miss a heavy strike, then immediately counter-attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Short Combos">
    Do not rely on long, complex combos. Stick to reliable 3-hit light attack strings followed by a safe dash-away to reset your positioning.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Stamina Management">
Avoid spamming dashes and heavy attacks. Running out of stamina leaves you temporarily defenseless and unable to block incoming strikes.
</Callout>

</Section>
